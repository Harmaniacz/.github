<h1>Harmony by Harmaniacz</h1>
<p>Harmony connects users with new friends through the language of music.</p>
<p>It is built by a team of 5 Software Engineering students from uOttawa as part of the SEG 2900 course.</p>
<p>The techstack included HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Figma, and Git.</p>
<p>The team was one of three selected from 28 groups to pitch the startup concept to CEOs and Founders within the Ottawa region.
<p>Harmony was awarded 2nd in both the audiences' and judges' categories.</p>
